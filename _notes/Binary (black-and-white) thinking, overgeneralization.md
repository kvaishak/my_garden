title: Binary (black-and-white) thinking, overgeneralization
---

Good-evil, smart-stupid, beautiful-ugly, tall-short, fast-slow, and so on and on. We think in binary terms. Our language reflects that. And some people are stuck very hard in such thinking. The worst case of it is _all-or-nothing_ thinking, when any result other than the best is considered as a failure. It causes stress and depression in people. A similar flaw is _overgeneralization_. We put into the same category very broad types of information. Prejudice, labeling, stereotypes are all related to overgeneralization.
